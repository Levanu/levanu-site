Building from Brisbane  ·  AUKUS Pillar 2

About Leva Nu.

Leva Nu was founded to close the gap between mission intent and deployed machine. Autonomous mobile systems — drones, ground robots, surface vessels — fail at integration because the disciplines that build them rarely share a single source of design truth. We build software that does.

The company operates as an Australian Pty Ltd, headquartered in Brisbane, with a deliberate focus on AUKUS Pillar 2 advanced-capability programs and AU-aligned defence and dual-use customers.

Our product

Sture — mission-to-machine for autonomous mobile machines.

A validation and coordination layer for teams building drones, ground robots, and autonomous watercraft. Sture keeps the whole machine aligned as the design evolves — flagging integration failures at design time, when changes still cost hours instead of months.
